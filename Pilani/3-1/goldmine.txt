The objective of this game is to find the goldmine.
The player will use arrow keys and space to navigate and select boxes respectively.
Scores are calculated based on how many boxes a player has opened before finding a goldmine.
The position of a goldmine is randomized based on the system clock time.

To run this game, you will need - 
MASM611 emulator

To play this game, you will need - 
a clearly disturbed mind.
